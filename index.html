WHO AM I

I am the Associate Dean of the Faculty of Physics at University of Bialystok.
I am also a lecturer and a researcher in the Division of Astronomy
and Astrophysics which is part of the Faculty.

My research activities include theory and analysis of observations of accretion discs in AGNs and Galactic black hole X-ray binaries.

SCIENTIFIC INTERESTS

  • Normal (type 1), Weak Emission Lines (WLQ) and Broad Absorption Lines (BAL) quasars
  • Radiative processes and spectral analysis in opt.-UV range of AGNs
  • X-ray timing analysis of AGNs and compact accreting objects
  • Cherenkov Telescope Array
  • BLS1 and NLS1 galaxies
  • Tidal Distruption Events
  • High Energy Emission
  • Accretion models
